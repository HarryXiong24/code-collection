## Tricks

I found that the most important trick is to find the invariants first or found a relationship between the two pointers, then use the invariants to solve the problem.

And it is hard to give a general rule to found the invariants or the relationship between the two pointers, but we should always try to find the invariants or the relationship between the two pointers first.
